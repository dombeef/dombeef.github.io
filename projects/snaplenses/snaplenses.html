Snapchat Lenses

Project Description

In december 2017 Snapchat released a program called Lens Studio that allowed anyone to create custom Snapchat lenses that they can share with others. I messed around with it soon after it came out, and in March 2018 Snapchat contacted me over my thinking emoji lens and wanted to put it on their "lens carousel" so anyone who used snapchat for a week would be able to use my lens as a default lens.

Soon after that in May 2018 I was contacted from Snapchat for joining their "Official Lens Creator" program for direct access to beta software among other perks, its been a fun ride for what started off as just a fun hobby to make lenses to share with friends!

I could post a list of the lenses I've made here, but since that would be hard to update every time I make a new lens heres a link to my "Official Lens Creator" page that shows my most popular lenses and my total stats for all the lenses I've created.
